My journey

I am a former 3D animator turned programmer. I am currently taking a three-year program of Computer Programming & Analysis at Seneca College.

Now, I know what you are thinking. Why?

Well, I have always been interested in programming, even back when I was working as an animator. I used python to write multiple scripts at multiple companies I worked for, to automate certain repetitive tasks for myself and others. Some of the scripts are still being used today.

I became known by my ex-coworkers as a "guru" simply because I knew the very basics of coding. But I knew that I was far from that, and decided I wanted to go back to school to deepen my knowledge of programming to be able to build programs of a larger scale.
